The [Google Cyber Security Program](https://joshmadakor.tech/google) is designed to provide valuable, actionable knowledge for breaking into cyber security. With this program, Google acknowledges that, besides having a great resume or interview skills, you need other abilities such as consistency, self-presentation, written communication, and the ability to execute a job hunt.

Here are some features of the [Google Cyber Security Program](https://joshmadakor.tech/google):

- Breadth of Topics: The program covers a wide range of security topics, including coding in Python, job hunt preparation materials, and much more.
- Affordability: For just $49.99 per month (after [a free seven-day trial](https://joshmadakor.tech/google)), you get access to top-tier educational material presented by security professionals at Google.
- Comprehensiveness: The program delves into essential topics like cyber security frameworks, regulatory bodies, and much more.

## **Roadmap to Getting a Job in Cyber Security**

By following these steps and the [Google Cyber Security Program](https://joshmadakor.tech/google), you can increase your odds of landing a job in the cyber security field:

### **Your Resume**

You will need to create a high-quality resume that stands out to both human reviews and Application Tracking Systems (ATS). Use a resume template (like this one from the description) to format your resume appropriately, adding your own relevant information. The quality of your resume is critical in securing job interviews.

### **Your Experience**

In the cyber security field, experience is critical. But how do you gain experience before having your first job? A possible solution: become a content creator by implementing tasks and creating high-quality content around them. This content can then be showcased on your portfolio and your resume.

### **Your Interview Skills**

Improving your interview skills is crucial for job hunting success. A fantastic way to upskill is by listening to podcasts like Darknet Diaries and CyberWire Daily. In addition, use AI tools like ChatGPT to generate practice interview questions and responses.

Remember, there's always room for growth within every role, including the opportunity to shift more towards cyber security over time.
