Breaking into cybersecurity feels intimidating, but it doesn't have to be. This post
walks through a realistic first 90 days.

## Why start now

The demand for entry-level analysts keeps growing. You don't need to know everything —
you need momentum.

## A simple plan

1. Learn the fundamentals (networking, OS, security basics)
2. Build a home lab
3. Practice with real scenarios

Check out the [Cyber Range](/cyber) to practice hands-on.
