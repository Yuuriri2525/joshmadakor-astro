Hi there, budding cybersecurity enthusiasts! Wondering which certification can unlock the door to your dream job in cybersecurity? Well, we are going to create a tier list of the top 10 cybersecurity certifications today.

This ranking is based on these essential things:

- The average salary for jobs demanding specific certifications
- The frequency at which these certifications appear in different job advertisements on Indeed.

## **Why Do Cybersecurity Certifications Matter?**

Certifications play a significant part in securing a job. They are one of the many keys you need to unlock your dream job. They showcase your skills, commitment, and knowledge and make you a desirable candidate for employers. However, certifications demand considerable time, energy, and money, so it's crucial to choose wisely.

## **Analyzing The Top Certifications**

We have selected the top 13 certifications based on average salary and appearance frequency. You might think going for the highest paying or the most frequently appearing certification is the best approach, but there's more to consider.

For instance, Offensive Security Certified Professional (OSCP) tops the list for the highest salary, but it's arguably one of the most challenging certifications and has the lowest frequency of listings from our selection. On the other hand, Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP) frequently appears in job descriptions but isn't the best option for a newbie due to its experience requirement.

## **Choosing The Right Certification**

For beginners, it's sensible to opt for certifications that are more common and manageable. The CompTIA Security+ certification is a good starting point, as it has a respectable average salary and decent listing frequency.
