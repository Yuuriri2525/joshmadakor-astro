Hi everyone! Today, we’re diving into something super cool - a Cyber Security Internship Program. Have you ever wondered how these programs work or what you can learn from them? Well, let's explore together!

## **Why an Internship Program?**

The program was born out of the need to help students gain on-the-job experience while dealing with real technology and cyber security problems. It's an addition to a comprehensive Cyber Security course, providing hands-on experience and a chance to apply what you've learned in a real-world setting.

## **What You’ll Learn**

- **Cyber Security Theory:** You’ll start with the basics - what security threats, risks, and vulnerabilities are all about. Plus, learn important frameworks like NIST and GDPR.
- **Hands-On Labs:** Build and manage a security operations center in the cloud, and work on live attack simulations.
- **Job Hunt Skills:** We'll help you build a knockout resume and prepare you for interviews, so you’re ready to land your dream job.

## **How the Internship Works**

After finishing the [Cyber Security course](https://joshmadakor.tech/cyber) and passing a test, you can apply for the internship. As an intern, you'll help others in the course by answering their questions and troubleshooting. Your efforts are tracked, and you can earn cool titles based on how much you help!

## **Answering Your Questions**

A lot of you have had questions about the internship, and we want to answer them:

- **Is it paid?** No, the internship is not paid. It’s designed to offer valuable, real-world experience without the administrative hassle of managing payroll for interns.
- **Will employers care?** Yes! Experience, whether from a paid or unpaid internship, shows that you’ve applied your knowledge in real-world scenarios. It’s a big plus on your resume.
