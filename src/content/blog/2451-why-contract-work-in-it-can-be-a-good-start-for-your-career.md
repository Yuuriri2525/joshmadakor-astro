Hey buddies! Are you curious about what it's like to work in IT and cyber security? Well, you're in luck because today we're diving into the world of contract work and how it might just be the jumpstart your career needed!

## **Getting Into the World of Contract Work in IT**

Josh, an expert in IT and cyber security, shares his thoughts on why you might want to consider contract work, especially when you're just starting out. Here are some key points he made:

- **It’s Everywhere:** Lots of IT jobs are contract-based, meaning companies hire for certain projects.
- **Security Myths:** Full-time jobs aren't always more secure than contract positions. Josh has never been fired from a contract job!
- **Easier to Get:** Getting a contract job can be easier because there are fewer steps and less competition.
- **Variety is the Spice of Life:** Working different contract jobs can expose you to many areas, making you a stronger IT professional.

## **Why Consider Contract Work?**

There are lots of reasons why contract work could be a great choice:

- You get to work on various projects, which keeps things exciting and lets you learn a lot quickly!
- There's less competition for contract roles. This means you might get a job easier and faster.
- It's a fantastic way to gain a ton of experience in the field, making you very valuable to future employers.

## **How to Be a Superstar Contractor**

Want to make sure you're the type of contractor every company wants to keep around? Here's how:

- **Deliver Value:** Always look for ways to contribute more than what's expected.
- **Be Independent:** Companies love contractors who can work without needing too much guidance.
- **Focus on Your Role:** As a contractor, you'll have a specific role. Excelling in this can lead to more opportunities, either as a continued contractor or a full-time employee.
