Your resume has about six seconds to make an impression. Make them count.

## Lead with outcomes

Hiring managers skim. Put the result first, then the task — not the other way around.

### Before and after

- **Before:** "Responsible for monitoring security alerts."
- **After:** "Triaged 50+ daily alerts and cut false positives 30% by tuning detection rules."

## The five tips

1. Lead with outcomes, not duties
2. Quantify everything you can
3. Tailor to the job description
4. Keep it to one page early in your career
5. Proofread, then proofread again

## One last pass

Read it out loud before you submit. Your ear catches what your eye skips.
