Hi everyone! Today, we're going to share an incredible story of determination and growth from Ray's personal experience. Ray made a big leap from a customer service representative to a senior help desk analyst, and he's here to tell us all about his journey.

## **A Little About Ray**

Ray is a proud family man, married straight out of high school with three energetic kids. Before jumping into the IT world, Ray spent much of his career in customer service, providing a strong foundation for his future in help desk roles. Despite the challenges, Ray knew he needed a change to provide better for his family, and that's when he decided to dive into IT.

### **Getting Started with IT**

Ray's IT adventure began with a feeling many of us know all too well - nervousness. Ray explained how he would get cold and start shaking when faced with new challenges. But his determination to improve his family's life pushed him forward. He embarked on IT courses and faced interviews head-on, despite the nerves.

### **Key Steps on the Road to Success**

Ray started with the Google IT support professional course and later took more specialized IT courses, focusing on skills needed for help desk roles.

Applying for Jobs: Ray applied to between 40 and 60 jobs, highlighting the importance of perseverance. His well-prepared resume got him three interviews and a job offer that led to a $12K salary increase.

Facing Interviews: Interviews were tough due to Ray's nervousness, but preparation was key. Practicing interview questions and believing in himself helped Ray succeed.

## **Work at the Help Desk**

Ray shared insights into his daily life as a senior help desk analyst. His work involves handling inbound phone calls, managing tickets, and solving technical issues. The role requires a mix of technical knowledge, especially in Active Directory, and soft skills like communication and problem-solving.

### **Challenges and Learning on the Job**

One story Ray shared was a nerve-wracking mistake where he rebooted a server instead of a thin client, causing a temporary but impactful downtime. However, this mistake became a learning opportunity, teaching him the importance of understanding his work environment and the impact of his actions.

## **Advice for Aspiring IT Professionals**

Ray's story is inspiring, especially for those looking to make a career change or start in IT. Here are some pieces of advice he shared:

Stay Motivated: Your drive to change your situation can lead you to new opportunities.

Learn from Mistakes: Don't be afraid to face challenges. Mistakes are opportunities to learn.

Prepare for Interviews: Knowing potential interview questions and practicing your answers can reduce nervousness and improve your performance.

Ray's journey from being a nervous interviewee to a confident IT professional shows that with determination, preparation, and a willingness to learn from mistakes, anyone can make a significant career change. Remember, it's okay to start nervous, but with each step, you'll grow more confident and skilled.

## **Conclusion**

Ray's story is a testament to the fact that change is possible, no matter where you start. It reminds us that with hard work, a bit of courage, and the right guidance, we can achieve our career goals. Thank you, Ray, for sharing your journey with us and proving that it's never too late to chase your dreams.

Remember, everyone, "You can do it!" just like Ray did. Who knows? Maybe one day, we'll be sharing your success story here, too.
