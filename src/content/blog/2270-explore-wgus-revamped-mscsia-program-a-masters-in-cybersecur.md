## **The Cybersecurity Program Details**

The Masters program in Cybersecurity at WGU aligns with real-world requirements and government initiatives. Here are some of the alignments:

- The NICE framework, which guarantees that you're learning skills that are relevant today.
- The NSA's Center of Academic Excellence guidelines, ensuring the curriculum meets real-world needs.
- The CISSP common body of knowledge, recognized for security domain relevance in the market.
- The Government's DoD 8570 and DoD 8140 initiatives, which detail the certifications required for different levels of government work.

## **Comparison:Old Program vs. New Program**

A comparison of the old and new program shows that the reworked program aligns better with the needs of the industry. New courses have been added, while redundant ones have been removed. The changes in the curriculum reflect an increased focus on cloud security, risk and compliance, secure software design, and cybersecurity architecture, among others.
