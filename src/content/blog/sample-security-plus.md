Security+ is the certification most hiring managers recognize for entry-level security roles.
Here's how to pass it without wasting months.

## Know the exam

The SY0-701 covers five domains, weighted toward operations and incident response. Read the
official objectives once before you start studying — it's your map.

### Question styles

- Multiple choice (the bulk of the exam)
- Performance-based questions (PBQs) that simulate real tasks

## A 6-week plan

1. Weeks 1–2: Watch a full course end to end
2. Weeks 3–4: Read a study guide and take notes
3. Week 5: Drill practice questions until you're at 85%+
4. Week 6: Review weak domains and PBQs

> Don't memorize answers — understand *why* each wrong option is wrong.

## On exam day

Flag the long PBQs, answer the quick multiple choice first, then circle back. Manage the clock
and you'll have time for everything.
